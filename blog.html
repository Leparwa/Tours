Lake Bogoria

Lake Bogoria (formely lake Hannington) is a saline alkaline lake (salty water lake) that lies in a volcanic reqion of south of Lake Baringo, little north of Equator. Lake Bogoria is one of world's host of lesser flamingoes. The Lake was officially classified as the National on 29th of November, 1973 by Kenya Government. The Lake is about 10 M deep with 34km by 3.5km wide. its basin extend to almsot 7000 square Km.
